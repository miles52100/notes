## Debunking McKinsey report 
McKinsey research that 'found comapnies with diverse leadership teams performed better than rivals' was flawed and should not be 'relied on' academics have found.

The widely quoted studies suggested increasing racial and ethnic diversity at the exec level can improve a company's financial performance were called into question in research published 
in Econ Journal Watch, an academic journal.
See [here](https://econjwatch.org/articles/mckinsey-s-diversity-matters-delivers-wins-results-revisited)
Econ is published by the Fraser Inst. a Libertarian Canadian think tank. 
Part of the global Atlas Network of think tanks, including the Inst of Economic Affairs in Britain.

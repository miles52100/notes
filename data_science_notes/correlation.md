## Matthew's , Phi coefficient

Statistics refers to the Phi coefficient. The ML community refers to this as Matthews' correlation coefficient, MCC.

### What is MCC for?

It's considered a better metric for binary classification than F1 score.
See [here](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phi_coefficient).

### Generalisation to the multi-class case

This is known as the $R_K$ statistic.
